CreatINN Academy

Premium Learning Management System with student and admin portals, course workflows, application handling, and alumni tracking.

Context

CreatINN Academy needed a digital experience that felt premium enough for an education brand while still handling the practical work of applications, student progress, course management, and alumni tracking.

What I built

I designed an LMS platform with a student-facing portal, admin workflows, structured course pages, and support for application flows. The product has to earn trust quickly, so the interface leans into clarity, hierarchy, and polished interactions.

Why this approach

Next.js and TypeScript gave the app strong structure, Prisma kept the data layer clean, and NextAuth handled authenticated journeys. The goal was not just to make a dashboard, but to make the academy look and feel credible.

Role & scope

I worked on the product structure, LMS flows, and the presentation layer for both students and admins.

Tech: Next.js, TypeScript, Prisma, NextAuth

Key features

  • Student portal for course access, tracking, and academy engagement.
  • Admin workflow for managing applications, content, and operations.
  • Alumni and course structure that supports the academy’s full lifecycle.
  • A visual tone that helps the platform feel premium instead of generic.
